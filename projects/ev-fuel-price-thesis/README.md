# Master's Thesis: Fuel Prices & EV Demand (New Zealand)

## Title
The Effect of Rising Fuel Prices on Consumer Demand for Hybrid and Electric Vehicles in New Zealand: A Quantitative Analysis of Economic, Policy, and Behavioural Influences

## Scope
- BSNS9000 Industry Research Project (2025)
- 89-page thesis report
- Mixed-method approach: panel data, survey, and ownership trial

## Methods
- 2010-2024 panel comparison (NZ, Norway, China, USA)
- Consumer survey (N=103)
- 3-month real-world Nissan Leaf ownership trial
- Elasticity analysis, Granger causality, multiple regression, and ARIMA
