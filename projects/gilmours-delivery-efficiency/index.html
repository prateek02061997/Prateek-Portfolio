Gilmours - Delivery Efficiency Analysis

Comprehensive workflow analysis project aimed at reducing delivery delays by 20% through systematic bottleneck identification and process optimization.

Target Impact: 20% delay reduction
Presentation: 34 slides
Focus: Operations & Analytics

🎯 Objectives

  • Reduce delivery delays by minimum 20%
  • Map complete end-to-end delivery workflow
  • Quantify inefficiencies by stage and route
  • Propose data-driven process improvements

🔍 Methodology

  • Stakeholder Interviews: Conducted in-depth interviews with drivers, dispatchers, and warehouse staff
  • Workflow Mapping: Documented current delivery process from order receipt to final delivery
  • Delay Analysis: Quantified delays by process stage and route grouping
  • Root Cause Analysis: Identified key bottlenecks and their business impact
  • KPI Development: Created tracking metrics for ongoing performance monitoring

📊 Key Metrics Analyzed

  • On-time delivery rate (baseline vs. target)
  • Average delay minutes by stage (receiving, picking, loading, transit, delivery)
  • Route-level variance and consistency
  • Rework and redelivery frequency
  • Driver utilization and idle time

💡 Recommendations

  • Implement dynamic route optimization software
  • Establish real-time delivery tracking dashboard
  • Create standard operating procedures for high-delay stages
  • Pilot automated dispatch scheduling for peak periods
  • Build feedback mechanism from drivers to operations team

📈 Expected Impact

  • 20% reduction in average delivery delays
  • Improved customer satisfaction through reliable delivery windows
  • Reduced operational costs via optimized routing
  • Data-driven decision making with KPI dashboards

  • Process Mapping & Workflow Analysis
  • Stakeholder Interviews
  • Data Analysis (Excel/Power BI)
  • Root Cause Analysis
