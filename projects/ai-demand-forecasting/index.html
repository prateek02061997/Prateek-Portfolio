AI-Powered Retail Demand Forecasting System

Designed and validated a retail demand forecasting workflow that turned historical sales patterns into planning recommendations for inventory and replenishment decisions.

Year: 2026
Outcome: Forecast-led 15% stock waste reduction opportunity
Focus: Python • Forecasting • Explainable AI

Project Overview

This project focused on a real business problem: reducing inventory waste without increasing stockout risk. I framed the forecasting problem as a decision-support use case, built a Prophet-based model, and translated the outputs into planning guidance that non-technical stakeholders could use in weekly inventory reviews.

Objectives

  • Define a clear AI use case tied to inventory and planning outcomes
  • Forecast weekly demand with business-friendly confidence ranges
  • Support explainable recommendations for stock and replenishment decisions
  • Evaluate the model against measurable waste-reduction outcomes

Methodology

  • Use Case Definition: Mapped business pain points around stock waste, planning uncertainty, and replenishment timing
  • Data Preparation: Cleaned and aggregated historical sales data into a forecasting-ready structure
  • Model Build: Developed a Prophet-based time series workflow to capture seasonality and trend shifts
  • Validation: Compared forecast outputs against historical periods and planning expectations
  • Decision Framing: Translated forecasts into operational recommendations stakeholders could interpret easily

Technical Stack

  • Language: Python 3.8+
  • Core Libraries: Prophet, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib
  • Data Source: CSV with daily sales transactions
  • Evaluation Metrics: MAPE, RMSE, and business impact modelling for waste reduction

Key Results

  • 15% waste-reduction opportunity: Forecast-led planning identified a credible opportunity to reduce overstock situations
  • Explainable outputs: Trend and seasonality insights made recommendations easier to communicate to non-technical stakeholders
  • Decision support: Weekly planning scenarios gave clearer guidance for inventory actions
  • Reusable workflow: The model structure can be extended with promotions, holidays, and additional business signals

Data Format

Sample data structure in data/sample_demand.csv:

Date,Sales
2023-01-01,150
2023-01-02,165
2023-01-03,142
...

Implementation Highlights

  • Automatic changepoint detection for sales trend shifts
  • Custom seasonality settings for retail calendar (holidays, promotions)
  • Business-friendly outputs suitable for operational review
  • Clear path for integration into a broader AI-enabled planning workflow
